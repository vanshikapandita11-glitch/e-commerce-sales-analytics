# 📊 E-Commerce Sales & Customer Analytics

## 🔍 Project Overview

This project presents an end-to-end data analytics pipeline on a large-scale e-commerce dataset (100,000+ records), focusing on extracting business insights related to revenue trends, customer behavior, and product performance.

The workflow integrates SQL, Python, and Tableau to simulate a real-world data analytics use case.

---

## ⚙️ Tech Stack

* **SQL (MySQL)** – Data storage, schema design, and complex queries
* **Python (Pandas, Matplotlib)** – Data cleaning, transformation, and exploratory data analysis
* **Tableau** – Interactive dashboard creation and visualization

---

## 📁 Project Structure

* `notebook.ipynb` – Data loading, cleaning, merging, and EDA
* `final_dataset_sample.csv` – Sample dataset used for visualization
* `dashboard.png` – Snapshot of final Tableau dashboard
* `queries.sql` *(optional)* – SQL queries used for analysis

---

## 📊 Key Analyses Performed

* Monthly revenue trend analysis
* Customer purchase behavior by day of week
* Top revenue-generating product categories
* Delivery time analysis and logistics performance

---

## 💡 Key Insights

* Revenue showed strong growth with peak performance in late 2017, stabilizing in 2018
* Customer orders were highest during weekdays (Monday–Tuesday), indicating behavioral patterns
* Categories such as **Health & Beauty** and **Bed, Bath & Table** dominated revenue contribution
* Average delivery time was ~12 days, with significant outliers indicating logistical inefficiencies
